My greatest art piece is this gallery. Through my work, I strive to address humanity’s most pressing challenges, such as curing diseases and improving lives. I am committed to donating 90% of all proceeds from this gallery to research centers focused on solving these critical problems, transforming art into a catalyst for tangible solutions.

This gallery represents my belief that creativity can inspire hope, healing, and progress. By sharing my art, I aim to empower others to embrace their own creativity and join the collective effort to tackle the challenges we face. Together, through art and action, we can create a brighter, healthier future.


My creativity draws inspiration from the remarkable masterpieces I’ve admired, the infinite universe, and the intricate beauty of consciousness and its interactions with other conscious entities (:
